About the Health Analytics, Research and Policy Team

This project is brought to you by the Health Analytics, Research and Policy (HARP) team at the Durham Region Health Department. HARP is responsible for population health assessment, evaluation, research, and knowledge exchange within the Health Department. To see more reports on the health of Durham Region residents, please visit the Durham Region health stats page.

About the application

This is an open source project that uses Jekyll, WET and Chartist.js.
